Perfect Pints

Nth Wonder dairy-free vegan gelato is a group hug of our Italian heritage, cutting-edge food tech, and high quality natural ingredients. Cholesterol-free and trans fat-free, Nth Wonder has 47% less saturated fat and loads more fiber and minerals than dairy ice cream. Best of all, our pints are packed with all the creaminess you come to expect from traditional artisanal gelato. They’re 100% nonne-approved.

Wonder of the World

We make our gelato with kenari nuts hand-picked from the rainforests of Indonesia. Rich in protein and full of vitamins and minerals, kenari nuts help us achieve traditional Italian gelato’s signature dense, creamy, and buttery texture thanks to a high concentration of oleic acid, the same found in olive oil. Click below to learn more about the kenari nut’s health benefits, and why the kenari tree is nicknamed “the tree of hope.”
