Perfect Pints

Nth Wonder dairy-free vegan gelato is a group hug of our Italian heritage, cutting-edge food tech, and high quality natural ingredients. Cholesterol-free and trans fat-free, Nth Wonder has 47% less saturated fat and loads more fiber and minerals than dairy ice cream. Best of all, our pints are packed with all the creaminess you come to expect from traditional artisanal gelato. They’re 100% nonne-approved.

Save Trees, Eat Gelato

Our hottest summer day will be our children’s coldest summer day, and that’s scary. One of the biggest culprits is industrial farming, like cattle ranching, which causes 80% of tropical deforestation and 13% of the world’s emissions. And cutting down trees is a double-whammy: we take down our biggest ally in protecting our planet from greenhouse gasses while releasing more of those harmful gasses into the air.

We’re here to fix all that. We went deep into mother nature to find a nut that doesn’t require industrial farming, and then we developed a really cool technology to harness its power. The result is our totally cow-free farm-free Nth Wonder, the best gelato on earth, for earth.

Wonder of the World

We make our gelato with kenari nuts hand-picked from the rainforests of Indonesia. Rich in protein and full of vitamins and minerals, kenari nuts help us achieve traditional Italian gelato’s signature dense, creamy, and buttery texture thanks to a high concentration of oleic acid, the same found in olive oil.
