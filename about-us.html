About Us

As hippies, our mission is to save mother earth and all that she entrusts us with. As scientists, we’ve developed technologies that harness the full potential of nature to create foods better for everyone. As Italians, rich and creamy gelato defines childhood happiness, pure and simple. At Nth Wonder, we invite you to join our family of tree-huggers, dreamers, and gelato aficionados by taking your spoon and finding your way to the bottom of one of our pints.
